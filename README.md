# Job-Market-Salary-Dashboard

## Project Objective
I created the Job Market Salary Dashboard to explore and visualize salaries in the Data Science field. This dashboard makes it easy to see how salaries change based on things like experience level, job type, remote work options, role, and country. ## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the average salary in the job market?
- Which role is the highest paying job?
- How does salary change with experience level?
- Which employment type offers higher salaries?
- What is the distribution across salary bands?
- How are jobs distributed by remote, office, and hybrid work?
- Which countries offer higher average salaries?
- What are the top-paying roles for entry-level professionals?

## Insights
- Salaries increase significantly from entry level to expert level.
- Contract roles show higher salary potential at senior and expert levels.
- Remote jobs dominate the job market compared to office and hybrid roles.
- Most job records fall within the medium and high salary bands.
- Entry-level roles in leadership and engineering positions offer higher pay compared to other roles.
- Salary levels vary widely across countries, highlighting geographic pay differences.
